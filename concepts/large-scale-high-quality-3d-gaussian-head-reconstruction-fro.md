# Large-Scale High-Quality 3D Gaussian Head Reconstruction from Multi-View Captures

## 作者
Evangelos Ntavelis, Sean Wu, Mohamad Shahbazi, Fabio Maninchedda, Dmitry Kostiaev

## 摘要
We propose HeadsUp, a scalable feed-forward method for reconstructing high-quality 3D Gaussian heads from large-scale multi-camera setups. Our method employs an efficient encoder-decoder architecture that compresses input views into a compact latent representation. This latent representation is then decoded into a set of UV-parameterized 3D Gaussians anchored to a neutral head template.
